Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the personal health tracking application ("Second Brain") operated by Franck Rocca for personal use only.

Data Collection

The application collects health and fitness data from connected wearable devices (including but not limited to WHOOP, Oura Ring) solely for personal health tracking and analysis purposes.

Data Usage

All collected data is:

  • Used exclusively for personal health monitoring and analysis
  • Stored securely in private databases
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never sold or used for commercial purposes

Data Storage

Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Data Retention

Personal health data is retained indefinitely for long-term health trend analysis, unless deletion is requested.

Your Rights

As the sole user of this application, you have full control over your data, including the right to access, modify, or delete any stored information.
